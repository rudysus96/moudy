MOUDY Music Bot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

MOUDY Music Bot ("The Service") is committed to protecting user privacy. This policy outlines our data handling practices in compliance with Discord Developer Policy.

1. Data Collection

We adhere to a strict data-minimization principle. We collect only the following transient data necessary for functionality:

  • User IDs: To identify who requested a specific song or command.
  • Voice Channel IDs: To allow the bot to connect to the correct audio stream.
  • Guild (Server) IDs: To store server-specific configurations (e.g., volume settings).

2. Data Retention & Storage

The Service operates primarily in real-time. Operational data is stored in volatile memory (RAM) and is cleared when the bot session ends or leaves the voice channel. We do not maintain long-term databases of user message history, listening habits, or personal conversations.

3. Audio Privacy

MOUDY does NOT record, store, or analyze user voice data. The bot only processes audio streams from external providers (YouTube/Spotify) to play music to you. We do not listen to your conversations.

4. Third-Party Services

MOUDY utilizes official APIs from third-party platforms. By using The Service, you acknowledge that data related to search queries interacts with these platforms, subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact the bot owner directly via the Discord platform support server.
